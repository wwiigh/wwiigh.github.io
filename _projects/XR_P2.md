## Project Overview
**Herb Quest (尋藥之途)** is a VR RPG game. The player takes on the role of a protagonist on a journey to find a rare, magical herb to cure their father, who was injured in a monster attack. 

Through interactive dialogues with various NPCs, players must piece together scattered clues, beat the mounsters, and navigate the virtual world to complete their quest.

## Technical Highlights & My Role
This project was awarded the prize primarily for our integration of an AI-driven conversational system, allowing players to have dynamic, unscripted dialogues with NPCs.

**Project Highlights:**
* **AI NPCs**: Integrated Inworld AI to generate real-time, context-aware responses for characters. *(Implemented by my teammate)*

**My Core Responsibilities:**
* **VR Player Control and Interaction**: Use Oculus API to finish VR control in the game(walk, grab, etc).
* **Game Flow Control**: Design the game flow system, control the game event and animation transition.
* **Enemy AI**: A finite state machine to control the enemy's behavior.
